Medical Attendant Work Responsibilities

  • Sponging / Bathing
  • Bed Pan / Diaper Care
  • Serving Diets – as per schedule
  • Medicine Administration under family supervision
  • Vital Monitoring – BP / Sugar / Pulse / Respiration / Temp
    (*Case to Case basis – Digital Monitoring)
  • Assistance in Urination / Toileting
  • Preparing for Patient Meals (*Only Female Carers)
  • Keeping the Elder / Patient Surroundings Clean
  • Nebulization (*Case to Case basis)
  • Mobility via walker / wheelchair / hand holding
